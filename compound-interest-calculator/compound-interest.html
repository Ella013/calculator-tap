How Our Compound Interest Calculator Works

Our compound interest calculator helps you estimate your investment growth over time. Understanding your potential returns can help you make better financial decisions and plan for your future.

Regular and Additional Contributions

The calculator considers both your initial investment and any regular contributions you plan to make. You can set the frequency of your contributions (monthly, quarterly, or annually) to match your investment strategy.

Compound Frequency

Choose how often interest is compounded on your investment:

  • Daily: Interest is calculated and added to your principal 365 times per year
  • Monthly: Interest compounds 12 times per year
  • Quarterly: Interest compounds 4 times per year
  • Semi-annually: Interest compounds twice per year
  • Annually: Interest compounds once per year

Note: More frequent compounding typically results in higher returns over time, though the difference between monthly and daily compounding is usually minimal.
